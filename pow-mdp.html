markov decision processes for analyzing blockchain consensus protocols

Date

June - December 2019

Description

I am  studying incentives in blockchain consensus protocols using computational and game-theoretical methods. One example of such analysis is using Markov Decision Processes (MDPs) to compute best-responses for revenue-maximizing miners. 

This work has led to the paper "Selfish Behavior in the Tezos Proof-of-Stake Protocol," available here.
